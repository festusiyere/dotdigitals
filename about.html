WHO WE ARE?
Kehinde Fagamigbe
CEO
Well, let's start with the basics: I'm Kenny, I'm in my 20's, I'm a photographer. Okay, got that covered, let's move on to the good stuff! I love to laugh and do my best to make others laugh. I make puns, and the kind of jokes that are so-bad-they’re-good. I love mac-n-cheese & wine. (If that’s not class, I’m not sure what is)
One of my favorite parts of my job is you: the people I get to meet and work with. It is such an honor to be asked to capture important moments in people’s lives. To be asked to be a part of important events, hearing stories, witnessing love stories unfold, watching kids grow up, it is such a gift to call this my job!
